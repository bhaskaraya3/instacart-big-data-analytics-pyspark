# 🛒 Instacart Customer & Product Analytics (PySpark)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes over **37M+ Instacart orders** using **PySpark** to uncover customer behavior, product demand patterns, and actionable business insights.

The goal is to simulate **real-world retail decision-making** using data.

---

## Tech Stack

* PySpark (Big Data Processing)
* Python
* Jupyter Notebook
* Data Engineering + Analytics

---

## Business Questions & Insights

---

### 1. Which departments drive the most orders?

**Insight:**

* Produce dominates overall sales volume
* Fresh food categories contribute the majority of orders

**Business Decision:**

* Optimize supply chain for perishables
* Focus inventory planning on high-demand departments

---

### 2. Which aisles generate the highest customer traffic?

**Insight:**

* Fresh fruits and vegetables attract the most customers
* Essential categories drive store visits

**Business Decision:**

* Place high-traffic aisles strategically
* Cross-sell complementary products

---

### 3. What are the most in-demand products?

**Insight:**

* Top products are daily essentials (bananas, milk, fruits)
* Indicates habit-based purchasing behavior

**Business Decision:**

* Use these products in recommendation systems
* Create bundle offers for frequently bought items

---

### 4. How does order volume vary by day of the week?

**Insight:**

* Peak demand occurs on weekends
* Lowest activity mid-week

**Business Decision:**

* Increase staffing on weekends
* Run promotions on low-demand days

---

### 5. What are peak ordering hours?

**Insight:**

* Highest orders between **10 AM – 3 PM**

**Business Decision:**

* Optimize delivery slots during peak hours
* Schedule targeted ads during high-traffic periods

---

### 6. How are customers segmented based on behavior?

**Insight:**

* Customers fall into Frequent, Regular, and Occasional segments
* Regular users form the majority

**Business Decision:**

* Design targeted marketing strategies per segment
* Focus retention on frequent users

---

### 7. How does customer type affect basket size?

**Insight:**

* Occasional customers have larger basket sizes
* Frequent users place smaller but more frequent orders

**Business Decision:**

* Offer bulk discounts to occasional users
* Improve quick reorder experience for frequent users

---

### 8. Which departments have the highest reorder rates?

**Insight:**

* Dairy and beverages show the highest reorder rates
* Indicates habit-driven consumption

**Business Decision:**

* Introduce subscription or auto-reorder features
* Prioritize availability of these products

---

### 9. Does cart position impact reorder behavior?

**Insight:**

* Items added earlier in the cart have higher reorder probability

**Business Decision:**

* Show recommendations early in the shopping journey
* Optimize cart ranking algorithms

---

### 10. Are popular products always highly reordered?

**Insight:**

* High demand ≠ high loyalty
* Some products are frequently bought but not consistently reordered

**Business Decision:**

* Separate products into:

  * High demand (traffic drivers)
  * High loyalty (retention drivers)

---

## Key Takeaways

* Fresh products dominate the business
* Customer behavior varies significantly by segment
* Demand patterns follow strong weekly and hourly trends
* Reorder behavior reveals habit-driven consumption

---

## Business Impact

This analysis helps in:

* Inventory optimization
* Personalized recommendations
* Customer retention strategies
* Revenue growth through targeted decisions
